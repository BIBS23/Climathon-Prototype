Our Vision

We introduce the agritech startup "DevKissan" to better the lives of farmers and the planet. The extra harvest and any byproducts, such hay, can be sold here by farmers. To produce environmentally friendly goods, we employ these byproducts as our raw materials. We can lessen the environmental risks these byproducts bring with them in this way. To help farmers choose what to cultivate on their land, we give resources and personnel to analyse the topographic characteristics and climatic conditions on their land
